title: "Dexterous Teleoperation: Challenges and Opportunities"
abstract: "Robotics is advancing rapidly, yet today's robots still struggle to match the dexterity of humans. A promising path toward autonomous dexterous robots is through learning-based methods trained on data collected via teleoperation, where a human operator controls a robot in real time to demonstrate complex tasks. In this talk, I will present the current state of dexterous teleoperation research and propose the field’s biggest challenges and opportunities."
